My Hobby

Playing musical instruments is one of my greatest passions. I can play the guitar, bass guitar, keyboard, and drums. Music has become an important part of my life, especially because I serve as the Sound Director in our church, where I also operate and manage the sound system during worship services and special events.

I enjoy watching movies and documentaries, particularly those related to war, history, military strategy, biographies, and action films. These genres help me gain a deeper understanding of historical events, leadership, and different cultures while also providing entertainment.

Playing basketball is one of my favorite recreational activities. It helps me stay physically active, improve teamwork and discipline, and develop sportsmanship both on and off the court.

I love reading books and articles about different topics. Although I do not always finish every book I start, I enjoy learning new ideas and expanding my knowledge through reading whenever I have the opportunity.

I have a strong interest in lifelong learning. During my free time, I often study new skills and explore different fields, such as cooking, music production, leadership, technology, and other practical life skills. I believe that learning should never stop

I also enjoy spending quiet time reflecting, planning personal goals, and improving myself. I believe that using my free time wisely helps me become more productive and prepared for future opportunities.

I like exploring new experiences and challenging myself to learn things outside my comfort zone. Whether it is acquiring a new skill, trying a different hobby, or taking on new responsibilities, I enjoy opportunities that help me grow as a person.

Most of my free time is spent doing activities that allow me to relax while continuously developing my knowledge, creativity, leadership, and practical skills. These hobbies not only bring me enjoyment but also help shape my character and prepare me for both personal and professional growth.
